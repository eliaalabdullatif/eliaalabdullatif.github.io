Segway Vehicle Control System Design

Modeled and stabilized an inverted-pendulum Segway in MATLAB and Simulink using PID, full-state feedback, reduced-order pole placement, and Luenberger observers. The reduced-order controller required ~18× less peak torque and ~14× less peak power than full-state feedback while maintaining stability.

SOLIDWORKS · 2024 – Present

Three-Arm Bearing Puller

A fully constrained SOLIDWORKS assembly modeling a three-jaw bearing puller — the mechanic's tool used to remove press-fit gears and bearings from shafts. Built around a central threaded drive screw and three symmetric arms connected to a center yoke through bolted pivot joints. Demonstrates symmetric mates, threaded-component assembly, and fastener integration.

SOLIDWORKS · 2024 – Present

Articulated Robotic Arm with Parallel-Jaw Gripper

A multi-component articulated robotic arm mounted on a workbench fixture, integrating a parallel-jaw gripper modeled as a separate SOLIDWORKS sub-assembly. The gripper uses a four-bar linkage mechanism to keep the jaw faces parallel as they open and close, driven through synchronized pulleys. Demonstrates assembly hierarchy, sub-assembly integration, and width-mate constraints across multiple linkage joints.

SOLIDWORKS Sub-Assemblies Four-Bar Linkage Mechanism Design
